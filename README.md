This is my interpretation of the Snakes & Ladders darts game you find at places like Flight Club.
Working from the outside of the board to the inner bull, each section is worth 1, so any double is 1, and treble is 3, the outer bull is 5, and the inner bull is 6.
Throw a dart, enter your score and the player token will move. Continue this until all three of your darts have been thrown.
Play will then move to the next player.
You must score exactly to land on the last position.
